Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025

1. General Information

As Zatu, we attach great importance to the protection of your personal data. This privacy policy provides information about the information we collect when using our platform and how we use this information.

2. Information Collected

When registering on our platform, we collect the following information:

  • First and last name
  • Phone number
  • Date of birth
  • Province and district information
  • Building and floor information

3. Use of Information

The collected information is used only for disaster preparedness purposes:

  • Neighborhood-based grouping
  • Emergency notifications
  • Coordination with AFAD
  • Statistical analyses (anonymous)

4. Data Security

All your data is stored encrypted with Zatu technology and kept on secure servers.

5. Data Sharing

Your personal data may only be shared with official institutions such as AFAD for disaster preparedness purposes. It is not shared with any third parties for commercial purposes.

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, delete, and object to your data.
