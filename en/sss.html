Frequently Asked Questions

What is National Disaster Network?

National Disaster Network is Turkey's national disaster preparedness network. It helps you be prepared for disasters by connecting with your neighbors beforehand.

How can I register?

You can register in 2 minutes by clicking the "BE PART OF THE NETWORK" button on the homepage. You just need to enter your basic information and select your city.

Is my data safe?

Yes, all your data is securely protected with National Disaster Network technology. It is processed in compliance with GDPR and used only for disaster preparedness purposes.

How does AFAD integration work?

The National Disaster Network platform works integrated with AFAD. In emergencies, coordination is provided with official institutions and notifications are shared.

Is it free?

Yes, the National Disaster Network platform is completely free. We believe that disaster preparedness should be accessible to everyone.
