About Us

What is Zatu?

Zatu is Turkey's national disaster preparedness network. Our goal is to prepare our citizens for disasters and strengthen neighborhood solidarity.

Our Mission

In major disasters, official help cannot reach everyone. Based on this reality, we support neighborhood-based organizations and strengthen communication between neighbors.

Our Vision

To create an active disaster preparedness network in every province of Turkey and ensure that every citizen is prepared for disasters.

How We Work

The Zatu platform allows users to join groups based on city and neighborhood. It works in coordination with official institutions through AFAD integration and provides rapid coordination in emergencies.
