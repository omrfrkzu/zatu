Why We Exist?

The Reality of Disasters in Turkey

Being prepared is not a choice, it's a necessity

Statistical Facts

On February 6, 2023, systematic search and rescue could not begin in the first 48 hours. Mobile communication and power outages occurred. Only 30.7% of AFAD personnel are search and rescue technicians. 42,310 lives lost; 448,018 evacuations.

Real data

Psychological State

How many families do you know in your building? Who will you communicate with in a disaster? Will you be able to find out if your loved ones are safe? Do you know the nearest gathering point?

Human-centered

Technical Problems

Mobile communication and power outages. Emergency VSAT terminals were sent to the region. Some airports closed due to damage. Emergency lines were opened for urgent communication.

Communication critical

Social Reality

One neighbor can affect an average of 8 people. Neighborhood-based organizations are most effective. Community solidarity saves lives. Those who know each other beforehand coordinate faster.

Community solidarity
