GDPR Disclosure

This is a disclosure text in accordance with the Personal Data Protection Law No. 6698.

Data Controller

As the Zatu platform, your personal data is processed in accordance with the Personal Data Protection Law No. 6698 ("GDPR").

Processed Personal Data

Personal data we process on our platform:

  • Identity Information: First name, last name, date of birth
  • Contact Information: Phone number
  • Location Information: Province, district, neighborhood, building and floor information
  • Technical Data: IP address, device information, usage data

Purpose of Data Processing

Your personal data is processed for the following purposes:

  • Creating a disaster preparedness network
  • Neighborhood-based grouping
  • Sending emergency notifications
  • Providing coordination with AFAD
  • Providing platform services

Data Transfer

Your personal data may be shared with official institutions such as AFAD for disaster preparedness purposes. It is not shared with third parties for commercial purposes.

Legal Basis for Data Processing

Your personal data is processed under the legal bases of "public interest" and "legitimate interest" within the scope of Article 5 of GDPR.

Your Rights

In accordance with Article 11 of GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Learn whether your personal data is being processed
  • Request information if processed
  • Learn the purpose of processing and whether it is used in accordance with its purpose
  • Know the third parties to whom it is transferred domestically or abroad
  • Request correction if processed incompletely or incorrectly
  • Request deletion or destruction
  • Object to the emergence of a result against the person by analyzing the processed data exclusively through automated systems

Application Method

To exercise your rights, you can send an email to info@zatu.org or apply in writing.
