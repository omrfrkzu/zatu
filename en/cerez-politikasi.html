Cookie Policy

Last Updated: 2025

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files saved to your device when you visit websites. The Zatu platform uses cookies to improve our services.

2. Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Cookies necessary for the platform to function:

  • Session Cookies: Maintains your session when you log in
  • Security Cookies: Provides secure connection

Performance Cookies

Used to analyze platform performance:

  • Page view statistics
  • User behavior analysis

Functional Cookies

To improve user experience:

  • Language preference
  • Theme preference (light/dark mode)

3. Managing Cookies

You can manage or delete cookies from your browser settings. However, some cookies are essential for the platform to function properly.

4. Third-Party Cookies

The Zatu platform may use trusted third-party services for analysis and security purposes. These services are subject to their own cookie policies.

5. Cookie Durations

  • Session Cookies: Deleted when the browser is closed
  • Persistent Cookies: Stored for a certain period (usually 1 year)
